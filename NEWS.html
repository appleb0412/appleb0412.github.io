The Hard Truth

Date: October 28, 2024
News from DAILYAPPLE

In 2023, suicide claimed around 740,000 lives globally, revealing a harsh reality: those who choose to end their lives often inflict deep pain on their families and communities. This decision is not just a personal struggle; it’s profoundly selfish.

In many countries, suicide rates among adolescents rose by 10% last year, despite the availability of resources like hotlines and therapy. Many individuals succumb to despair rather than reach out for help.

While mental health issues are real, we must hold those who choose suicide accountable for the devastation they leave behind.
